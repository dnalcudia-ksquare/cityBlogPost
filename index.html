Campeche, Camp.

Campeche is a port city of Mexico, located on the Gulf of Mexico.

History

On October 4, 1540, Campeche was the first town founded by the Spanish in the Yucatan peninsula.

Before the Spanish conquest, the area that Campeche currently occupies was populated by the Mayan civilization.
